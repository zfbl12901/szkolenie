## üöÄ Quick Start

### Step 1: Create a Free AWS Account

1. Go to: https://aws.amazon.com/free/
2. Click "Create a Free Account"
3. Fill out the form (credit card required but not charged)
4. Verify your identity by phone
5. Choose a support plan (free)

**Important:** AWS won't charge you as long as you stay within Free Tier limits.

### Step 2: Explore Free Tier

**Free services useful for Data Analyst:**

- **Amazon S3** : 5 GB storage (always free)
- **AWS Glue** : 10,000 objects/month (always free)
- **Amazon Redshift** : 2 months free (750 hours)
- **AWS Lambda** : 1 million requests/month (always free)
- **Amazon Athena** : 10 GB data scanned/month (always free)

### Step 3: Follow the Training

1. Start with module 1 (Getting Started)
2. Follow module order
3. Practice with module 7 projects
4. Monitor your usage in AWS Billing

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Cost Management

### Tips to Stay Free

1. **Monitor Billing**
   - Enable billing alerts
   - Regularly check AWS Cost Explorer
   - Recommended limit: $5 alert

2. **Respect Free Tier Limits**
   - Read conditions carefully
   - Some services free for 12 months
   - Others always free (with limits)

3. **Delete Unused Resources**
   - Stop unused instances
   - Delete empty S3 buckets
   - Regular cleanup

4. **Use Free Regions**
   - Some regions offer more free services
   - Check availability by region
